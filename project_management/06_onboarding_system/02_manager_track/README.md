# Manager Track

**Purpose**: Task execution management and quality assurance
**Prerequisites**: Complete 00_foundation module
**Role Scope**: Sprint delivery, quality control, team coordination

## Learning Objectives

Manager agents will master:
- Sprint planning and task breakdown
- Progress tracking and delivery management
- Quality assurance and testing protocols
- Team coordination and communication
- Stakeholder reporting and status updates

## Track Structure

### task_management/
Sprint execution and delivery coordination
- Task breakdown and estimation techniques
- Progress tracking and velocity measurement
- Dependency management and critical path analysis
- Delivery coordination and release management

### quality_control/
Testing standards and validation processes
- Test-driven development practices
- Code review protocols and standards
- Quality gate enforcement and validation
- Defect tracking and resolution management

### reporting_standards/
Communication and status management
- Sprint reporting and metrics collection
- Stakeholder communication protocols
- Issue escalation and resolution tracking
- Documentation standards and maintenance

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Sprint Execution**: Ensure timely delivery of sprint objectives
2. **Quality Assurance**: Maintain code quality and testing standards
3. **Team Coordination**: Facilitate communication and remove blockers
4. **Progress Tracking**: Monitor velocity and identify risks early
5. **Stakeholder Updates**: Provide clear status and manage expectations

## Success Metrics

- Consistent sprint delivery and velocity
- High code quality with minimal defects
- Proactive identification and resolution of blockers
- Clear and timely stakeholder communication
- Continuous improvement in team processes

## Common Scenarios

### Sprint Management
- Daily standup facilitation and progress tracking
- Impediment identification and removal
- Scope adjustment and change management
- Sprint retrospectives and improvement planning

### Quality Assurance
- Test plan development and execution
- Code review coordination and standards enforcement
- Defect triage and resolution tracking
- Release validation and deployment coordination

### Stakeholder Communication
- Status report generation and distribution
- Risk communication and mitigation planning
- Change request evaluation and impact analysis
- Success metrics reporting and trend analysis

## Daily Workflows

### Morning Routine
1. Review overnight automated test results
2. Check for critical issues or blockers
3. Prepare daily standup agenda and updates
4. Coordinate with orchestrator on priorities

### Execution Management
1. Track task progress against sprint goals
2. Identify and address impediments quickly
3. Facilitate team communication and coordination
4. Monitor quality metrics and gate criteria

### End of Day
1. Update progress tracking and metrics
2. Prepare status reports for stakeholders
3. Plan next day priorities and coordination
4. Document any issues or decisions made

## Validation Criteria

- [ ] Can effectively plan and execute sprints
- [ ] Demonstrates strong quality assurance practices
- [ ] Maintains clear communication with all stakeholders
- [ ] Proactively identifies and resolves blockers
- [ ] Delivers consistent results within sprint timeframes

## Tools Mastery

- Project tracking systems and metrics dashboards
- Automated testing frameworks and CI/CD pipelines
- Communication platforms and reporting tools
- Quality assurance tools and code review systems

## Escalation Guidelines

Managers escalate to:
- Orchestrators for strategic decisions and resource needs
- Technical leads for architecture or design questions
- Stakeholders for scope changes or timeline adjustments
- Quality team for standards clarification or exceptions
