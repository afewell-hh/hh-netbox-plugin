# Project Planning Directory

**Purpose**: Strategic planning, roadmaps, and future work planning for the HNP project.

## Key Documents

- **strategic_roadmap.md** - Long-term vision and major milestones
- **resource_planning.md** - Capacity planning and resource allocation strategies
- **risk_management.md** - Identified risks and mitigation strategies
- **milestone_schedule.md** - Major deliverable timeline

## Subdirectories

### sprint_planning/
- **active/** - Current sprint planning documents
- **sprint_template.md** - Template for sprint planning

## Planning Process

1. **Strategic Planning** - Quarterly roadmap reviews
2. **Sprint Planning** - Bi-weekly sprint planning sessions
3. **Resource Planning** - Monthly capacity reviews
4. **Risk Assessment** - Continuous risk identification and mitigation

## Templates

Use templates from `/99_templates/` for:
- Sprint planning documents
- Risk assessment forms
- Resource allocation plans
